About TubeSnap

TubeSnap is your AI-powered companion for transforming long YouTube videos into quick, digestible insights.

Why we built this

Time is precious. Whether it's lectures, interviews, or trending topics—TubeSnap extracts key takeaways instantly so you stay informed without the bloat.

How it works

Paste a YouTube link → We fetch and process the transcript → Our smart AI generates a short, coherent summary for you.
